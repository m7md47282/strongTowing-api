About Strong Towing Services

Our Story

Founded in 2009, Strong Towing Services has grown from a small local business to one of the most trusted towing companies in the region. What started as a single truck operation has evolved into a comprehensive fleet serving customers across three states.

Our commitment to excellence, reliability, and customer satisfaction has earned us the trust of thousands of customers and partnerships with major insurance companies and automotive clubs.
